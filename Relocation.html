Relocation Project.

This program allows you to input a yearly salary and a zip-code. It then will return the current averages of that zip-code. It will also show how much you will pay in taxes in this location. It will also add in a few other things for the location like current local gas prices and more. Finally it will let you know how much money you will have left at the end of each month.



This is just the Beta version.

You will notice the numbers will be off a little since the 401K and the rest of the benefits are not include into this.

There is an app currently being developed to access this information and more. The app will have more features along with more customization to give a 99% accuracy.
